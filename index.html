As I stroll through the vibrant corridors of the University of California, Riverside, I am constantly reminded of the transformative power of media. My name is Yubin Jo, and I am a senior student pursuing a major in Media at UCR. My journey through the realms of media has been nothing short of exhilarating, filled with moments of discovery, creativity, and personal growth.

Living in the bustling metropolis of Southern California, I was immersed in a world where media played a central role in shaping narratives and influencing perspectives. From the silver screen to the digital realm, I was captivated by the myriad ways in which stories were told and experiences were shared. It was this early fascination that sparked a fire within me, propelling me towards a future where I could harness the power of media to effect positive change. When I arrived at UCR, I was eager to dive headfirst into the world of media studies. From the moment I stepped onto campus, I was welcomed into a community of scholars, creators, and visionaries, each driven by a shared passion for storytelling and communication. With the guidance of dedicated professors and the support of my peers, I embarked on a journey of exploration and self-discovery that would shape the course of my academic and professional pursuits. In the classroom, I have delved deep into the theoretical underpinnings of media, studying everything from the history of cinema to the ethics of journalism. Armed with this foundational knowledge, I have eagerly embraced opportunities to apply my learning in real-world settings, whether through hands-on production projects or immersive internships with industry-leading organizations.

Outside of academia, I have sought out opportunities to engage with media in all its forms, from filmmaking and photography to digital media production and multimedia storytelling. Whether I'm behind the camera capturing moments of raw emotion or editing footage to create compelling narratives, I find immense joy and fulfillment in the creative process. Through my work, I strive to amplify diverse voices, challenge societal norms, and spark meaningful conversations about issues that matter. As I approach the final chapter of my undergraduate journey, I am filled with a sense of excitement and possibility for the future. Armed with a wealth of knowledge, a diverse skill set, and a deep passion for media, I am confident in my ability to make a meaningful impact in whatever path I choose to pursue. Whether I find myself working in film and television production, investigative journalism, digital media marketing, or media education, one thing is certain: my journey as a UCR senior with a Media major has equipped me with the tools and the mindset to navigate the ever-evolving landscape of media with confidence and purpose.
